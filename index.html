Week 1

The first week was great and we spent most of the time getting situated. We started on Tuesday May 28th by getting our accounts and information situated. We meet the rest of the mentors and participants in the program and got started working on our assignments. We read an article about how to read a research paper and then got started learning about Probabalistic Road Maps. We spent the rest of Tuesday reading the articles and answering the discussion questions which we then finished on Wednesday. On Thursday May 30th we got started working on the basic design of our websites. We then went to lunch and when we came back we began downloading and running the vizmo and PMPL files. On Friday May 31 we spent the morning working with the XML files and changing the paramaters to better understand the methods. We looked at different PMPL algorithms such as Uniform Random Free, Gauss, OBPRM and MAPRM and how changing certain paramaters would increase runtime, nodes and collisions. We then had a meeting run by Diane to discuss the information in the first part of the crash course. This discussion marked the end of the first lesson.
